Who We Are

Affordable Housing Shouldn't Mean Compromise

At Riverstone, we believe Affordable Housing Shouldn't Mean Compromise. Our philosophy replaces "settling" with individual dignity, providing high-quality homes that serve as a foundation for a better quality of life. By ensuring the local workforce can afford to stay, we drive community growth and economic resilience. We don't just build to a price point; we build the stability every community needs to thrive.

What We Do

Comprehensive Real Estate Development Expertise

Riverstone focuses solely on developing affordable, attainable and workforce housing communities. We mitigate and manage risk throughout the development lifecycle of an affordable housing project, from site identification and conception, through financing and development and ongoing asset management.

Creative Financing

Innovative capital structures leveraging tax credits, public-private partnerships, and mission-driven investment to make affordable housing projects financially viable and sustainable.

Construction & Delivery

Efficient construction methods and smart material sourcing that deliver quality homes at attainable price points.

Community Partnerships

Working with municipalities, employers, and nonprofits to structure programs that unlock housing opportunities for families, seniors, veterans and the disabled.

Our Values

Principles That Build More Than Homes

01

Attainability First

Every design decision, every material choice, every partnership is evaluated through the lens of keeping homes attainable and affordable.

02

Quality Without Exception

Affordable doesn't mean cheaply built. Our homes are designed and constructed to the same standards as market-rate developments.

03

Community-Centered

We build neighborhoods, not just houses. Our developments include green spaces, walkability, and connection to schools and services.

04

Sustainable Growth

Energy-efficient designs and responsible land use ensure our communities are built to last for generations, not just quarterly returns.
