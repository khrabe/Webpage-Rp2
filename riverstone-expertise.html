Our Expertise

30+ years of dedicated experience in developing, financing, and managing high-quality affordable housing solutions.

Riverstone has extensive experience in the housing development sector, boasting 30+ years of dedicated experience in developing, financing, and managing high-quality, sustainable, and critically needed housing solutions. Our unwavering commitment to addressing affordable and attainable housing needs has driven our successful completion of over 1,000 projects developed and/or financed, encompassing a diverse portfolio of developments from duplexes to large-scale multi-family communities and senior living facilities in urban and rural markets.

Our expertise spans the entire development lifecycle, from initial site acquisition and meticulous due diligence to complex financial structuring utilizing a broad array of public and private funding sources. We possess a proven track record of navigating intricate regulatory landscapes and forging strong partnerships with government agencies, non-profit organizations, community stakeholders, and financial institutions to ensure project viability and maximize community impact.
